What is this Website all about?



This website is developed to work as a simple engine to help you to find a tution teacher in your locality.   
	It is simple to use and it gives you a list of tutors and the subject that they offer to teach. 
All you need to do is to create an account as per the category to which you belong (student/teacher).
	During account creation just give in the relevant information and there you go.
All that's left for you to do now is to login into your account and begin the search....!!!
